Speaking Tips

Improving your speaking skills requires practice and confidence. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Practice regularly: Set aside time each day to practice speaking.
  • Record yourself: Listening to your recordings can help you identify areas for improvement.
  • Learn phrases: Memorize common phrases to use in conversations.
  • Engage in conversations: Practice with native speakers or fellow learners.

Interactive Speaking Activities

Role Play

Choose a scenario and practice speaking as if you are in that situation. Record your conversation and listen to improve.

Describe a Picture

Look at a picture and describe what you see in as much detail as possible. This helps build vocabulary and fluency.

Conversation Prompts

Use provided prompts to start a conversation. Practice responding naturally and keeping the conversation flowing.
