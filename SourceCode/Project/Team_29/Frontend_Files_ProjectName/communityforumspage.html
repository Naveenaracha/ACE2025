Community Guidelines

Our community is a place to support and encourage one another in language learning. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Be respectful and courteous to others.
  • Stay on topic and contribute positively to discussions.
  • Avoid using offensive language or sharing inappropriate content.
  • Report any violations of these guidelines to the moderators.
