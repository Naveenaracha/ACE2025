Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I track my progress?

You can track your progress by visiting the 'Progress Tracking' page. Here you can see your completed lessons, words learned, and streak information.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team by filling out the contact form below. We will get back to you as soon as possible.
